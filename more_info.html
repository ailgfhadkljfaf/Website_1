More Information

Kards is a free-to-play World War II-themed digital collectible card game developed by 1939 Games. Players can build decks using cards from various nations, including Germany, Great Britain, the Soviet Union, the United States, and Imperial Japan. The objective is to destroy the enemy's headquarters on the battlefield, which is divided into three fronts: the player's support line, front line, and the enemy's support line. The game features turn-based gameplay with strategic elements, allowing players to employ tactics such as bombing, digging in for defense, and calling in naval support.
